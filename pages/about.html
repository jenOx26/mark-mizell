About Mark

Mark was born in Illinois and spent a large part of his adult life in Tennessee. Mark began his legal career in 1996 in Nashville, Tennessee and spent 18 years litigating matters throughout the middle Tennessee region. Over the course of his 26 year legal career, he has practiced and excelled in a wide range of legal areas and litigated cases in Courts ranging from Municipal Court to the US Court of Appeals and everything in between.

In Tennessee Mark ran his own firm for some time prior to moving, but as it usually happens, 9 years ago he fell in love with the Colorado lifestyle while visiting and relocated to the Colorado Springs area.

One of the biggest joys Mark takes from work is explaining not just what is occurring in someone’s legal matter, but why. It’s important that his client’s have a thorough understanding of the law and how this affects there lives as they move through the legal process.

In his time away from the office Mark enjoys a wide range of activities including biking, hiking, kayaking, scuba diving, skiing, and snowshoeing.
